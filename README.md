## 📖 The Story Behind the Project
This project was born out of a desire to create something special, engaging, and personalized for my student, **Ibrahim**. Traditional tasbih counters are great, but I wanted to build an interactive, modern web application that he could use anywhere—whether on his computer or his phone. 

To make his learning experience more interactive, I designed the UI to look and feel like a real 3D physical tasbih device. It includes his name to make it personal, customizable colors so he can pick his favorite, and a built-in library of short Dhikr with their Arabic text, transliteration, and English meanings to help him learn. 

Finally, I integrated a live Firebase backend so that his progress syncs instantly across devices in real-time!

## ✨ Features
* **Personalized 3D UI:** A highly interactive, realistic-looking digital device.
* **Custom Themes:** 5 color swatches (Blue, Red, Black, White, Green) that smoothly transition the entire device's appearance.
* **Dhikr Library:** Select different prayers to display beautiful, textbook-quality Arabic script alongside meanings.
* **Live Synchronization:** Connected to a Realtime Database so progress on one device is instantly mirrored on another.
* **Haptic Feedback:** Vibrates on supported mobile devices to simulate physical button clicks.

## 🤫 Secret Reset Instructions
To prevent the counter from being reset accidentally, the reset buttons are completely hidden! Here is how to restart the counter back to zero:

### On a Computer (PC/Mac)
Simply type the word **`reset`** (`r` - `e` - `s` - `e` - `t`) anywhere on your keyboard while the page is open.

### On a Mobile Device (Phone/Tablet)
You must tap the screen in a secret 5-step sequence:
1. Tap the **Top-Left** corner of the screen.
2. Tap the **Top-Right** corner.
3. Tap the **Bottom-Right** corner.
4. Tap the **Bottom-Left** corner.
5. Tap directly in the **Center** of the screen.

*(This ensures the counter is never accidentally cleared while holding the phone!)*
